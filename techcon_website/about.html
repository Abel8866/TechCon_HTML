History

TechCon began as a small meetup in 2016 where local developers shared practical tools and workflows. Over the years it evolved into a multi-day conference featuring international speakers, hands-on workshops, and community-led sessions.

From modest beginnings, TechCon expanded its program to include tracks on web development, machine learning, DevOps, and design, attracting attendees from industry and academia.

Mission

Our mission is to connect practitioners and leaders to share actionable insights, foster collaboration, and promote ethical use of technology. We prioritize inclusive programming, practical workshops, and networking opportunities.
